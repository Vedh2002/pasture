Welcome to PASTURE CHICKEN

"At PASTURE CHICKEN, we believe in raising our chickens in a way thats sustainable, ethical, and nourishing for both the animals and our customers. Founded with a passion for quality and a commitment to the environment, our farm is dedicated to providing delicious, pasture-raised chicken that you can trust."

Our Philosophy

We take pride in our holistic farming practices that prioritize the well-being of our birds. Our chickens roam freely on lush pastures, enjoying a natural diet and fresh air. To enhance their quality of life, we utilize mobile coops that allow us to rotate our flock to fresh ground regularly. This not only improves their health and happiness but also enriches the soil and promotes a balanced ecosystem.

Our Commitment to Quality

From the moment they hatch to the time they reach your table, our chickens are cared for with the utmost attention. We avoid antibiotics and hormones, ensuring that our products are as wholesome as possible. Every bird is raised with love and respect, reflecting our commitment to sustainable agriculture.

No Antibiotics Ever

At PASTURE CHICKEN, we are committed to raising our chickens without the use of antibiotics, ensuring that our products are as wholesome and natural as possible. This philosophy stems from our dedication to animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and your health. Our approach prioritizes the health of our flock through proper care, management, and habitat.

Mobile Coops (Rotated Daily)

Our mobile coops allow chickens to enjoy the freedom of foraging in fresh pastures every day. By rotating the coops regularly, we ensure that our birds have access to new, nutrient-rich grasses and insects, promoting natural behaviors and a healthier lifestyle. This daily movement not only enhances their physical well-being but also contributes to the superior flavor and quality of our chicken.

Fresh Grass and Bugs Daily

Our chickens are free to roam on lush pastures, where they can engage in their instinctual foraging behaviors. They delight in pecking at fresh grass, seeds, and a variety of insects, which not only satisfies their natural instincts but also provides a diverse and balanced diet. This daily foraging enhances their overall well-being and leads to flavorful, nutrient-rich meat.

Our Story

At PASTURE CHICKEN, our journey began with a simple yet powerful belief: that the way we raise our food matters. Founded by a passionate team of farming enthusiasts, our mission is rooted in a commitment to sustainable practices and the welfare of our animals. It all started on a small plot of land, where we envisioned a better way to raise chickens. Inspired by traditional farming methods and the importance of ethical treatment, we implemented mobile coops that allow our birds to roam freely on fresh pastures. This innovative approach not only enhances their health and happiness but also revitalizes the land we cherish. As we nurtured our flock, we witnessed the profound connection between the animals, the land, and the people who consume our products. Each chicken is treated with care and respect, reflecting our dedication to quality and sustainability. Our commitment extends beyond just raising chickens; we strive to build a community of like-minded individuals who value the importance of knowing where their food comes from.

Know Where Your Food Comes From

In a world filled with food choices, knowing where your food comes from is more important than ever. At PASTURE CHICKEN, we believe transparency is key to fostering trust and connection between consumers and their food. When you choose PASTURE CHICKEN, you’re not just selecting a product; you’re embracing a philosophy. We are proud to share every step of our journey, from our pasture to your plate. Our chickens are raised in mobile coops, allowing them to roam freely in lush pastures, ensuring they live a healthy and fulfilling life.

What Our Chickens Eat

At PASTURE CHICKEN, we believe that a healthy diet is essential for raising happy, thriving chickens. That’s why we prioritize a natural and balanced diet that supports their well-being and enhances the quality of our meat.

A Natural Forage Diet

Our chickens enjoy a diverse diet primarily consisting of fresh grasses, seeds, and insects found in their pasture. This natural foraging behavior not only keeps them active and healthy but also contributes to the rich flavor and nutritional value of our chicken.

No Antibiotics Ever

At PASTURE CHICKEN, we are committed to raising our chickens without the use of antibiotics, ensuring that our products are as wholesome and natural as possible. This philosophy stems from our dedication to animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and your health.

Why No Antibiotics?

Antibiotics are often used in conventional farming to promote growth and prevent disease, but we believe that true animal husbandry starts with prevention rather than intervention. By providing our chickens with a natural environment to roam freely and a balanced diet, we minimize the risk of illness and promote their overall well-being.

Healthy Chickens, Healthy Food

Our approach prioritizes the health of our flock through proper care, management, and habitat. Healthy chickens raised in stress-free environments are less susceptible to disease, allowing us to raise them without the need for antibiotics. This not only leads to better animal welfare but also results in meat that is free from any antibiotic residues.
